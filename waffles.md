# Waffles

## Ingredients
- 250g flour
- 10g of baking powder
- 40g of sugar
- 2 eggs
- 500g of milk
- 100g of melted butter
- 1 dash of salt

## Preparation

- Mix wet ingredients first (Milk, eggs, butter and sugar)
- Dry ingredients next (Flour, baking powder, salt)
- Mix to smooth batter.

Ideally wait 1h before cooking.
