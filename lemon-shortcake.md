# Lemon Shortcake

## Ingredients
- 90g olive oil
- 200g sugar
- 50g milk
- 15g Amaretto liquor
- 4 eggs
- 1 lemon, zested and juiced
- 5g magic powder
- 50g of almond flour
- 150g of wheat flour
- 1 dash of salt

## Preparation

- Mix to smooth batter (wet ingredients > dry)

# Cooking
- 50 mins at 350f
